FFR is a tool designed to ease the pains of finding a group to play with in Final Fantasy XIV. Featuring a responsive interface and a wide array of tags for both the softcore and hardcore crowds, forming a group of like-minded players from your phone or desktop has never been easier.

 Here's a short list of features FFR offers that FFXIV's Party Finder does not:

   • Participate in other in-game activities while your party fills.

   • Host and join multiple parties simultaneously.

   • Schedule raids and recruit for parties ahead of time.

   • Expand your search to include multiple datacenters.

   • See who is in a party and what other jobs they're willing to play before you join.

   • Pinpoint parties you're interested in through a wider array of search parameters.

   • Form larger parties for The Baldesian Arsenal and other in-game events.
